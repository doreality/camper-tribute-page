Steve Jobs

The man who inspired a generation

He said ...

  • "Stay hungry, stay foolish."

    This quote emphasizes the importance of curiosity, learning, and taking risks in order to achieve great things.

  • "Innovation distinguishes between a leader and a follower."

    Jobs believed that innovation was key to success and that true leaders are those who have the ability to think differently and create new solutions.

  • "Your work is going to fill a large part of your life, and the only way to be truly satisfied is to do what you believe is great work."

    Jobs encouraged people to pursue their passions and do meaningful work that they are passionate about.

Here's a time line of Steve Jobs' life:

  • 1955 - Born on February 24 in San Francisco, California.
  • 1976 - Co-founded Apple Computer Inc. (now Apple Inc.) with Steve Wozniak in his parents' garage.
  • 1984 - Introduced the Macintosh, the first commercially successful personal computer with a graphical user interface.
  • 1985 - Left Apple after a power struggle with the company's board of directors.
  • 1986 - Founded NeXT Inc., a computer platform development company.
  • 1986 - Acquired The Graphics Group, which later became Pixar Animation Studios.
  • 1997 - Apple acquired NeXT Inc., bringing Steve Jobs back to the company he co-founded.
  • 1998 - Introduced the iMac, a groundbreaking all-in-one computer that revitalized Apple's product line.
  • 2001 - Launched the iPod, a portable digital music player that revolutionized the music industry.
  • 2003 - Apple opened the iTunes Store, a digital media platform for music, movies, and TV shows.
  • 2007 - Introduced the iPhone, a revolutionary touchscreen smartphone that redefined the mobile industry.
  • 2010 - Introduced the iPad, a tablet computer that transformed the way people consume media and use technology.
  • 2011 - Resigned as CEO of Apple but continued as Chairman of the Board.
  • 2011 - Passed away on October 5, leaving behind a lasting legacy in the technology industry.
